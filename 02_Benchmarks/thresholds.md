# VISTA BENCHMARKS — cited thresholds (makes scores defensible)

all numbers here are SOURCED. use in criteria so scores not arbitrary.

## technical SEO / Core Web Vitals (source: Google web.dev, 2025-26)
measured at 75th percentile of real users.
LCP (load) good = <=2.5s | needs work 2.6-4s | poor >4s
INP (responsiveness) good = <=200ms | 201-500 | poor >500
CLS (visual stability) good = <=0.1 | 0.11-0.25 | poor >0.25
note: only ~48% mobile sites pass all 3. passing = above-median.
CWV ~10-15% of ranking weight.

## local SEO / GBP (sources: Whitespark 2026, Localo 2M-profile study, WebFX)
primary category = #1 local ranking factor.
review count bands (Localo):
  <10 reviews = weak
  10+ = ranking lift begins (WebFX)
  150+ = pos 11-20 avg
  200+ = top-3 local pack correlation (avg ~250)
complete GBP = 7x more clicks than incomplete.
rating: 4.0+ trust threshold. positive ratings improve local rank (Google).

## GEO — how AI picks sources (sources: Princeton/GT/AllenAI KDD2024, Yext 6.8M citations)
top 3 GEO methods that boost citation:
  add expert quotes = +41% citation probability
  add statistics = +30%
  cite sources = +30%
Yext citation mix: 44% first-party sites, 42% listings, 86% brand-manageable.
=> brand CAN control most of what AI cites. first-party site matters.
AI-Google source overlap dropped 70%->under 20% (Brandlight). AI has own prefs.

## AEO — answer format (sources: HubSpot, Surmado, Frase, void.ma, DemandSage)
direct answer = 40-60 words right after a question heading.
schema impact: pages w/ schema 2.4x more likely in AI results (DemandSage).
  58% higher snippet visibility (Netguru).
inverted pyramid: answer first, detail after.
~60% of Google searches now zero-click. visibility must be IN the answer.
priority AEO actions (80% of result): FAQPage schema + snippet-optimized pages + E-E-A-T.

## social engagement (sources: Mosseri Jan2025, Emplifi 2026, RivalIQ, Hootsuite, Sprout)
CRITICAL: engagement definitions vary 10x across sources. VISTA locks ONE (see scoring-math).
IG ranking signals (Mosseri confirmed Jan 2025), in order:
  1. watch time (highest, video)
  2. sends per reach (DM shares) = 3-5x value of likes for new-audience reach
  3. likes per reach
shares > saves > comments > likes for algorithmic value.
IG "good" ER context-dependent. RivalIQ median all-industry ~0.36-0.50% (by-follower method).
  automotive IG ~4.8% (per-post method, DemandLocal). METHODS NOT COMPARABLE.
  use: 1%+ by RivalIQ method = top 25%. VISTA uses locked weighted formula, benchmark vs sector.
original content = 40-60% more reach than reposts (Mosseri).
10+ reposts in 30 days = excluded from recommendations.
posting: 3-5x/week quality > daily mediocre.
follower count = "nearly meaningless" post 2024-25 algo shift. RATIO is truth.
