## ECOSYSTEM MAP (fill before scoring, mandatory)
map discourse platforms for THIS market+industry before scoring RC.
SL automotive example: AutoLanka forum, EV Users FB group, Millionaires Paradise FB, SL auto YouTube, Google Reviews.
sentiment criteria: code min 20 discourse items. score on volume not cherry-pick.
local-language sampling is MANDATORY where the market has significant non-English discourse (for Sri Lanka: Sinhala and Tamil). an English-only sample in a multilingual market is a biased sample and invalidates the sentiment criteria.
dark social (WhatsApp, Viber, Telegram, private FB groups) is structurally unobservable from outside. VISTA cannot measure it. every report MUST carry the standing disclosure line specified in 00_README under deliverables. never imply the RC score covers private discourse.
