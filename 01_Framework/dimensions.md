# VISTA 6 DIMENSIONS — boundaries + attribution

## master attribution rule
every evidence piece → exactly ONE dimension, by dominant function.
never scored twice.
- brand-owned publishing → SC
- 3rd party editorial / expert → BA
- user/customer/enthusiast discourse → RC
- brand's own answers to buyer Qs → AV (AEO)
- how answers experienced on brand site → DE
- whether brand shows up when searched → SV

## D1 SV — Search Visibility (100)
def: how easily customer finds brand via search engines + maps, conventional queries.
owns: organic rank, technical SEO, local SEO, GBP, site architecture, backlinks-as-rank-signal, rich results.
NOT: AI answers (AV), editorial-as-credibility (BA), content storytelling (SC).

## D2 AV — AI Visibility (100)
def: how well brand surfaces in AI answers (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Claude, AI Overviews) when customer researches.
owns: brand mention freq+quality in AI, source citations, buyer-Q answers on owned surface, structured content feeding AI.
NOT: traditional rank (SV), FAQ page UX (DE), 3rd party editorial AI cites (BA gets source credit, AV gets visibility).

## D3 DE — Digital Experience (100)
def: quality of brand's owned digital env for a buyer researching purchase.
owns: website UX, info architecture, mobile, performance, purchase journey, lead capture, ownership content architecture.
NOT: content story (SC), whether site ranks (SV), whether buyer Qs answered in content (AV).

## D4 BA — Brand Authority (100)
def: volume + quality of independent 3rd party credibility signals in public domain.
owns: editorial, thought leadership, exec interviews, awards, expert commentary, authoritative backlinks, corporate parent signals.
NOT: customer reviews (RC), owner discourse (RC), brand-owned publishing (SC).

## D5 SC — Social & Content (100)
def: strategic execution + quality of brand-OWNED publishing across social + content channels.
owns: owned social (IG/FB/LI/YT/TikTok), owned blogs/hubs, visual identity, cadence, storytelling, content mix, video strategy.
NOT: 3rd party editorial (BA), UGC (RC), whether social ranks in search (SV).

## D6 RC — Reputation & Community (100)
def: what 3rd parties/customers/owners/communities say in public. living discourse brand does not control.
owns: sentiment, reviews, forum discussion, owner testimonials, UGC, advocacy, ambassadors, community presence, crisis response.
NOT: brand-owned publishing (SC), 3rd party editorial (BA), reviews-as-SEO-signal (SV).

## edge case rule
when evidence could fit 2 dimensions: attribute to dominant function.
examples:
- editorial backlink: rank uplift→SV, source credibility→BA. count once, dominant.
- GBP review: rank signal→SV, sentiment→RC. attribute RC (humans read it more than it moves rank).
- brand FAQ page: the answers→AV, page usability→DE.
- owner post brand reshares: original→RC, the reshare→SC.
- indep automotive journalist YT review→BA. general lifestyle vlogger in car→RC.
